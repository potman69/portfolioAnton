About

I’ve always loved computers (in the broad spectrum) and gaming. I’ve been building and repairing computers on and off for 15 Years. My best friend is a Computer Developer and he once asked me if it would be something I would be interested in doing. Not knowing what he meant, he wrote code and “hello world” appeared on the screen. I was fascinated and I wanted to know everything.

I have only been coding for about a year and a half including my year at Codex. Coding has changed my outlook on life and even how I implement daily tasks. Coding is a very love/hate relationship, but the hate is all worth the love and wouldn’t change it for anything. I look forward to the future and what it holds.
